This is an H1 Heading

The H1 heading is the largest and used for main titles.

This is an H2 Heading

The H2 heading is smaller and used for major section titles.

This is an H3 Heading

The H3 heading is smaller and used for subsections.

This is an H4 Heading

The H4 heading is smaller still and used for sub-subsections.

This is an H5 Heading

The H5 heading is even smaller and used for less important sections.

This is an H6 Heading

The H6 heading is the smallest and used for the least important titles.
